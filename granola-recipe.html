History of Granola

Granola has been the most ignored,if i can least say about it,cerial in many people's breakfast menu.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 tsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp cinnamonr
  • 1/3 cup sliced almonds
  • 2 Tbsp sunflower seeds
  • 1 Tbsp flax seeds
  • 2 Tbsp honey (or maple syrup)
  • 2 Tbsp shredded coconut

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients very well in a bowl.
  2. Toast this mixture in a non-stick pan on the stove at medium heat, stirring consistently for 3-5 minutes.
  3. Let the granola cool off and serve with fruit and yogurt/milk of choice!.

Notes

  1. To make it chunkier:use 1/2 oats and add a bit more honey (about 1/2 tbsp). Once the mixture is heated, it will caramelize and form clusters.
  2. Gluten-free option:use gluten-free oats
  3. Vegan option:use maple syrup instead of honey
  4. Paleo option:don't add oats, use some more sunflower seeds and almonds instead.
